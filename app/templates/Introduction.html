New York is one of the most populous cities in the world, with a vast transportation system, including very high demand for taxi service.


The portfolio focuses on the demand for taxi service in New York city, including Uber, lyft and other for-hire-vehicles. Thanks to TLC website(Taxi and Limousin Commission), on which the taxi trip record in New York is available to the public. It provides the passenger pick-up point, destination and time records of New York taxi and other for-hire-vehicles in the past 6 years.


The goal is to predict densidy of taxi service demand in New York that varies with location and time. Specifictly, given a location, date and time period, are we able to predict the number of pick-ups in that location?


Demand prediction is of significant use for the public.
For taxi companies, it provides data-based stretagies to allocate cars into different location in specific time period so as to maximize the utilization rate.
For traffic planners, damand prediction is an important tool to assist traffic management on specific day, time and location.




Machine learning methods will be utilized to do demand perdiction given location, time of day, and will be futher improved by incooperating features like climate data, vacations etc.
